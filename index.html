Our Kaupapa

The Physics Association of the University of Auckland exists to build a welcoming, inclusive community for physics students of all backgrounds. We aim to support students academically, socially, and professionally through events, workshops, and opportunities to connect with staff, industry, and each other — all while having a bit of fun along the way.
